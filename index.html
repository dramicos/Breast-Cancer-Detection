THIS IS NOT ACTUAL MEDICICAL ADVICE !
THIS IS A PROJECT MADE BY STUDENTS OF THE UNIVERSITY OF MINNESOTA.
THIS IS SPECIFICALLY TO RESEARCH HOW TO DETECT BREAST CANCER.

Our group set out to research which machine learning technique was most likely to provide the correct diagnosis for True Positives and False Negatives. We wanted to make sure that the people who need care can go and get the care they need. To do this we tested a number of different techniques and ran the best performer(s) through a confusion matrix. The results you see on this website are the results we got from those tests.
